• Complete your design by selecting materials for all non-building squares. To do so, click on a square and select the material for that square from the options that pop-up.
  • When you are ready to test your design, click on "Test Design."
  • You will be asked "how much did it rain (in inches)?" In the textbox, inserts a value for rainfall in inches (such as 1, 1.2, 2, or another number value).
  • Press enter on your keboard or click the checkmark next to the textbox to test your design.
