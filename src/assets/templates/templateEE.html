• Click on the yellow tile to select a surface material.
  • When you are ready to test the computational model, click on "Test Model."
  • You will be asked "how much did it rain (in inches)?" In the textbox, insert a value for rainfall in inches (such as 1, 1.2, 2, or another number value).
  • Press “Enter” on your keyboard or click the checkmark next to the textbox to test the model.
  • Click on “Test History” to see all your results.
